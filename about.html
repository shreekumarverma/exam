About ExamPane

ExamPane is a lightweight, modern web-based platform designed to help students take multiple-choice quizzes easily. Built with Bootstrap, it features a sleek interface, timed exams, result tracking, and dark mode for comfortable use at any time of day.

Whether you're preparing for your final exams, brushing up on computer science basics, or just challenging yourself, ExamPane has the tools to support your learning journey.
